# Customer Discovery and Pilot — Nashrino

**Effective:** 2026-07-16
**Research cadence:** Every 2 weeks, 4–8 participants per round
**Related issues:** #340 (recruit participants), #344 (Round 1), #348 (Round 2), #352 (pilot launch)

---

## Provisional customer definition

Persian-speaking social-media managers, freelancers, and small teams that professionally manage one to five Instagram business or creator accounts.

**Screening criteria (all 6 required):**
1. Manages Instagram as a job, not only as a hobby
2. Publishes at least 3 times per week
3. Handles comments or DMs weekly
4. Uses at least 2 tools, spreadsheets, chats, or manual processes
5. Has approval, reporting, coordination, or response-accountability needs
6. Can influence or make a software purchase decision

## Usability rules

- Maximum 5 tasks per participant session
- Tasks must have a clear success condition
- Do not tell the participant which menu to use
- Record: completion, time, assistance given, errors, confidence (1–5), SEQ (1–7)
- Use the same benchmark tasks across rounds for comparability
- Include at least 1 mobile participant per round
- Include at least 1 keyboard/assistive-technology review before paid launch
